When should I arrive?

Please ensure you arrive at St Peter’s Church by 1215 for our 1230 ceremony. We really don't want you to glimpse the bride walking up the hill so please don't be late!

Where should I park for the ceremony?

Parking is limited at the Church. If you are able to walk up the hill, please consider parking on Dark Lane. If you are driving to the church, please share lifts where possible.

Where should I park for the reception?

There is loads of parking at Rhyse Farm.

Do I need wellies for the farm?

No! Rhyse Farm is no longer a working farm. You’ll be fine in your smart shoes, but we won’t object if you want to slip into something more comfortable to dance the night away!

Will you be feeding us?

Yes! There will be a grazing feast when you arrive at Rhyse Farm, at around 1430. If you’re like us, we’d recommend a good brunch before the ceremony.

There will be other, more different food, later in the afternoon with speeches and there will be food in the evening!

We can cater for all dietary requirements, please let us know about any in your RSVP.

Will there be drinks?

Of course! We’ll be providing some welcome drinks, and there will be unlimited tea and coffee until midnight. Other drinks will be offered by Rhyse Farm’s team.

Are little ones welcome?

Yes! We’d love to see your family, but we are limited by numbers. If they’re on the invite they’re definitely welcome, if we’ve missed anyone, please let us know!

If there’s a new little one we didn’t know about; Congratulations! We already know of a few new arrivals who will be attending, you won’t be alone as a new parent!

But, of course, if you want a child-free evening, we’re happy to be the excuse to leave them with the babysitter!

Can I bring my partner?

We are limited on numbers but we want to be as inclusive as possible. Please get in touch if you would like to bring someone not included on your invite.

When do I need to RSVP?

Please RSVP as soon as possible, at the latest by 9th June.

Your password is on your invite.

Where should I stay?

There is space on Saturday night for both van and tent camping at Rhyse Farm for £10. Please let us know on your RSVP if you would like to camp.

There are plenty of local hotels and accommodation.

Can I bring confetti?

You can absolutely bring confetti as long as it's biodegradable!

Can I leave my car at Rhyse Farm?

Yes, but please ensure you collect it before 11:00am on Sunday the 10th.

What time does the party end?

Carriages at midnight!

We recommend pre-booking transport back to your accommodation.

I have another question?

Please let us know if you any have other questions.

We'll update this list with further questions when we think of them!
